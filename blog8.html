My Final Thoughts on the Senior Seminar Class


To be honest, I didn't think I would be as dejected as I am about this class ending, but I will definitely miss it.

I think a really important aspect of this class, at least what I enjoyed mostly from it, was the daily discussions of the current events that
everyone finds interesting and brings to class, and how important it is to know what is going on and what people are focused and worried about.
To have your "finger on the pulse" with that other people are interested in will help with everyone getting on the same page with starting put the class discussions.

Another topic I want to highlight for this class is the insights I have for the discussions for case studies and examples of the
CompSci world of working, and I wish I had a dedicated notebook for those sections to look back while I am focusing more and more on
breaking into the CS job market and keeping those points discussed in class in mind.

The last thing I want to discuss for this class is the importance of putting passion into what you look into for this class. This is the last
lecture class I have for my college experience, and in because of that fact its the last branch of freedom I think I can express in my work, and to
have that drive of passion for what I can talk about in this last class is something I do not take for granted.

Overall ok, would recommend.
